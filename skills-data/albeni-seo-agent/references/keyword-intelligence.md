## Priority Framework

- **P1 — High Intent (BOFU)**: Keywords with commercial/transactional intent, high conversion potential. Target with both organic content AND paid ads. Examples: "merino wool t-shirt", "t-shirt sotto giacca uomo", "cut and sew merino"
- **P2 — Mid Intent (MOFU)**: Keywords with comparative/problem-solving intent. Target with educational content and retargeting. Examples: "merino vs cotton", "best fabric for t-shirt", "t-shirt under blazer"
- **P3 — Topical Authority (TOFU)**: Broad informational keywords that build domain authority. Target with content pillars, no paid spend. Examples: "smart casual men", "capsule wardrobe men", "italian clothing brands"
